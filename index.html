Introduction to LogLink

LogLink allows you to quickly and easily send text, images and locations from mobile apps (eg Telegram or WhatsApp) directly to your Logseq or Obsidian graph.

Demo

Supported mobile apps

LogLink currently supports:

  • Telegram (beta)

Future plans:

  • Email

  • Signal

Shelved plans:

  • Whatsapp (no longer under development)

See the full roadmap for more details.
